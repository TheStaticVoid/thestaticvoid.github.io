Additonal Crafting Info

Below is a list of the changes in crafting that are worth mentioning. Things like the universal dyeing work for all dyeable items, but I only included on image for the sake of space.

Craftable Notch Apples

Re-adds the classic "Gapple" crafting recipe that Mojang unjustly removed from the game in 1.9(?). Time to slay some Pigmen!


Craftable Horse Armor

Works with Iron and Gold Horse Armor crafting as well. Leather Horse Armor is craftable in vanilla, therefore it's recipe is unchanged.


Stairs to Blocks

Allows for the reverse crafting of any type of stairs back into blocks. Conversion rate is three stairs for one block.


Slabs to Blocks

Similar to the stairs to blocks recipe, this allows for the conversion of two slabs into a full block. Works for all slabs.


Blue Ice to Packed Ice

Allows for the reverse crafting of Blue Ice into Packed Ice. Conversion:
1 Blue Ice -> 9 Packed Ice.


Packed Ice to Ice

Similar to above, allows for the reverse crafting from Packed Ice to regular Ice. Conversion:
1 Packed Ice -> 9 Ice.


Nether Wart Block to Item

Continuing the trend from above, allows for 1 Nether Wart Block into 9 Nether Wart.


Wool to String

Reverse crafting from Wool blocks to 4 String.


Universal Dyeing

Allows for dyeing of already dyed blocks into any other color. A feature missing from vanilla Minecraft for some reason.
