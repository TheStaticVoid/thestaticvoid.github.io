Rules

Rules aren't fun, we all can agree on that, but to ensure an enjoyable experience for all players, there are some simple rules I expect everyone to follow.

  1. No griefing. Yes, Factions is included in the server, but it does not mean you can grief someone else's base.

  2. Do not abuse any exploits, bugs, or glitches in Minecraft for any gain.
    NO CHEATING / CHEAT CLIENTS

  3. Use of derogatory terms on the basis of race are not allowed. Just be civil in your chats.

  4. Combat logging and teleporting out of combat are not allowed, even if you are the one being attacked first.

  5. Faction wars should be kept to a minimum, only resulting from failing to compromise in a civil way.

  6. Base raids are only allowed when members of a faction are currently online. Offline raiders will be punished.
