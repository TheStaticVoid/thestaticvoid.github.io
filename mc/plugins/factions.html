Factions Info

If you are familiar with the old version of the Factions plugin, you might as well throw that knowledge out the door. This plugin is an extension of the original plugin, but with a lot new added features and significant changes in the operation of the plugin.
This plugin allows for claiming chunks and creating groups to protect your valuables from being disturbed by others.
By default, each player contributes 30 power to his/her faction. This means one person can claim 30 chunks for themselves.
Innapropriate faction names will be removed.

/f — Allows for access to the basic factions menu. I highly recommend scanning through this and seeing what is available for you to use. /f map — Shows all claims in a radius around you. You can click the unclaimed regions to claim them for your own faction. Please do not click too fast, otherwise you could get kicked for "spamming." /f inv username — to invite your friends to your faction. They can accept your invite by using: /f join factionname
