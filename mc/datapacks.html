Datapack List

Any datapack that can't be explained in a few lines of text have their own dedicated pages that you can reach by clicking on the name of the datapack.

Some datapacks have been removed due to poor performance and causing extreme server slowdowns. You can view those removed datapacks here.

BlazeAndCave's Advancements Pack

BlazeandCave's advancements pack is one of the first advancement datapacks ever created and is also one of the largest and most feature-packed. It adds over 600 new advancements to your world so you have some extra challenges to do in your survival world.

Adds materials smelting from the regular furnace. This datapack allows you to upgrade your furnaces to replace them entirely.

Elevators

Again, anoter datapack inspired by a mod. Place a Carpet, of any colour, on a Note Block to create an Elevator. Jump or crouch to travel vertically to another Elevator within 16 blocks.

Powering an elevator with redstone will deactivate it.

Hammerhead

Drop an Iron block and an Iron/Diamond pick/shovel on top of an enchantment table while you have 10 levels to enchant it with the ability to mine a 3x3 area. Works omnidirectionally. Pick breaks cobble, stone, netherrack, end stone, diorite, andesite, granite, and polished variants. Shovel breaks dirt, sand, grass, podzol, red sand, gravel and coarse dirt.
